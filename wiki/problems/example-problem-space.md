# Make Reading Compound Into Ideas

## Why This Matters

A research repository should do more than preserve what has already been learned. It should also make promising ideas easier to revisit, pressure-test, and connect to later evidence.

## Current Understanding

Keeping raw sources separate from derived notes, durable concepts, and idea-carrying problem spaces creates a better environment for compounding understanding than one-off retrieval from unstructured files.
