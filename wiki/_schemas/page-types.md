# Page Types

## Source Notes

Purpose:
- Derived from one raw source.
- Capture summary, claims, embedded thought entries, and explainable links.

Stored in:
- `wiki/sources/`

## Concept Pages

Purpose:
- Hold durable synthesis that can survive beyond any single source.
- Aggregate multiple source references around one concept.

Stored in:
- `wiki/concepts/`

## Problem-Space Pages

Purpose:
- Represent a long-lived question space that incubates ideas.
- Connect related sources, concepts, and knowledge gaps.
- Stay pre-executional.

Stored in:
- `wiki/problems/`
