# Thought Promotion Rules

## Default

- A `Thought Entry` stays local to a source note unless it clears a promotion threshold.

## Promote a thought when

- It generalizes beyond the current source into a reusable concept.
- It sharpens or redirects a long-lived problem space.
- It reveals a concrete knowledge gap worth tracking in `Knowledge To Acquire Next`.
- It creates a meaningful cross-source bridge that is likely to matter again.

## Keep a thought local when

- It is just a reading reaction with no durable relevance.
- It restates the source summary without adding a new angle.
- It is too speculative to shape concepts or problem spaces yet.

## Required documentation

- When a thought is promoted, the source note should say where it was promoted and why.
- At least one example in the vault should show a local-only thought and a promoted thought side by side.
