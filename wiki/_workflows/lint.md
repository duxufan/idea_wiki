# Lint Workflow

## Purpose

Health-check the vault for structural and epistemic issues.

## Checks

- Orphan pages with no meaningful inbound links.
- Broken provenance between `wiki/sources/` notes and raw files.
- Unsupported claims that lack visible source grounding.
- Stale or weak cross-links with no explainable rationale.
- Problem spaces with no current knowledge gaps.
- Concepts that should perhaps be demoted or split.
- Recent raw sources that were registered but never ingested.

## Output

- Report findings by severity.
- Suggest which reading or synthesis action would most improve the vault next.
